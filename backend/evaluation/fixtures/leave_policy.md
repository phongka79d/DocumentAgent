# Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

Employees receive eighteen annual leave days each calendar year. The carryover authorization phrase is ORCHID-CARRY-6, allowing at most six unused days to move into the next year.

## Requests

Submit ordinary leave requests at least seven calendar days before the first day away. Emergency leave may be reported on the same day through the manager channel.

## Evidence Retention

Approved leave records remain available for thirty days under the leave archive rule.
